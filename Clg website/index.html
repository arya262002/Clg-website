Machine Learning

Machine learning is a branch of artificial intelligence where algorithms learn from data to make predictions or decisions. It involves training models to recognize patterns and generalize from examples, enabling tasks like image recognition, language translation, and autonomous driving.

  • Mr. Ashish Chandra, United Health Care (Healthcare Analytics Trends)
  • Dr. R. K. Singh, United Health Care (Healthcare Analytics Trends)
  • Mr. Hitkul, IIIT Delhi (Deep Learning Recipes)
  • Mr. Sandeep Gosai, Humonics Global (Transformers)
  • Dr. J Shukla, IIIT Delhi (Emotion Recognition from Physiological Signals)
  • Dr. Rajiv Tripathi, NIT Delhi (Shallow vs Deep Neural Network)
  • Dr. Reshma Khemchandani, SAU (Large Scale Learning Algorithms)
